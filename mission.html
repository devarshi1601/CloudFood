Our Mission

Our Primary Mission is to provide safe and wholesome food that is currently thrown away to the needy people and reduce food insecurity these days. In an survey it is claimed that every year more than 3 lakh children die due to hunger under the age of 5 years. So to overcome this big issue we CloudFood start this mission to save the food and distribute it to the needy people through our helping hands like NGOs , needy persons itself can take food from the website.
